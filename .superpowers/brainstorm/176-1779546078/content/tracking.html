How should order tracking work?

After placing an order, what does the customer see?

A

Live Tracking with WebSockets (Recommended)

Status updates push to the customer in real-time. Backend emits events via Socket.io as the order moves through stages. Feels exactly like Swiggy.

B

Simulated Progress (Auto-advance)

After placing an order, the status auto-advances through stages on a timer (e.g. Confirmed → Preparing → Out for delivery → Delivered). No WebSockets needed. Looks great in demos.
